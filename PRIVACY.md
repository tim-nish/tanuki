# Privacy Policy

Tanuki is a local development tool that runs entirely on your own machine.

- It spawns headless Claude sessions only against disposable clones of
  repositories you point it at.
- It collects **no** telemetry, usage data, or analytics.
- It has no backend and transmits **no** data to the author or any third
  party. All processing happens in temporary directories on your machine.
- Tanuki invokes Claude through your own Claude Code installation; that usage
  is governed by [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy),
  not by this plugin.

Tanuki does not track you or your work.
